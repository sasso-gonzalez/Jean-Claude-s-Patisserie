Jean-Claude’s Patisserie's

We are a family run business that has been established for over 30 years. We are based in Eze France and have been serving the local community with our delicious cakes and pastries. We are proud to say that we have been able to expand our business to the online world and are now able to deliver our products to your door. We are passionate about our products and we hope that you will enjoy them as much as we do.
